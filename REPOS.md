   A LangChain-powered agent that can take in a URL and generate Twitter & LinkedIn posts based on the content of the URL. It uses a human-in-the-loop (HITL) flow to handle authentication with different social media platforms, and to allow the user to make changes, or accept/reject the generated post.

   **Key Features:**
   - URL-based content generation: Automatically generates social media posts from any URL
   - Multi-platform support: Generates posts for both Twitter and LinkedIn
   - Human-in-the-loop: Allows users to review, edit, accept, or reject generated posts
   - Social media integration: Handles authentication and posting to Twitter and LinkedIn
   - Slack integration: Can ingest URLs from Slack channels and send updates
   - Cron job support: Automated scheduling for regular post generation
   - Customizable prompts: Easy to customize for different brands and content styles
